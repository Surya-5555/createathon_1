Safety Techniques

Technique 1: Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Always be mindful of your surroundings and the people around you. Avoid distractions like using your phone in unsafe areas.

Technique 2: Use Well-Lit Areas

Stick to well-lit and populated areas when walking at night. Avoid shortcuts through dark or secluded places.

Technique 3: Self-Defense Training

Consider taking self-defense classes to equip yourself with skills that could help you in dangerous situations.

Technique 4: Carry a Personal Alarm

Carry a personal alarm with you, which can be activated to attract attention if you feel threatened.

Technique 5: Trust Your Instincts

If something doesn't feel right, trust your instincts and remove yourself from the situation immediately.
